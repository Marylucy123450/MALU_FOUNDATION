About MALU_FOUNDATION

We are dedicated to making education accessible for all by supporting students through scholarships, mentorship, and community engagement.

Our Mission

To unlock the potential of underserved students across Kenya by providing access to quality education, mentorship, and sustainable opportunities that empower them to shape their own futures.

Our Vision

A future where every child in Kenya, regardless of background, has the tools, support, and confidence to become a changemaker in their community and beyond.

Meet Our Graduates

These are just a few of the many success stories of students who have graduated through the support of MALU Foundation. They are now making an impact in their communities and beyond.

Fancy Atieno - Software Engineering Graduate, Moringa School

Fancy was able to pursue her dreams of becoming a software engineer thanks to the MALU Foundation's scholarship program. The mentorship and financial support she received helped her gain the skills and confidence to build her career. Today, Fancy is working toward her goal of becoming a front-end developer, contributing to the tech industry and inspiring others in her community to follow their passions.

Enos Omenda - Law Graduate, Kenyatta University

Enos Omenda's journey to becoming a lawyer was made possible through the generous support of MALU Foundation. The foundation not only provided financial assistance but also mentorship and guidance, ensuring she had the resources to thrive in her studies at Kenyatta University. Today, Enos is using her legal expertise to advocate for the rights of the underprivileged, becoming an inspiration to many in her community.
